"Sharing is Caring"

We're a community organized group that connects people and their excess food together to share with each other

It's simple, all you need to do is

  • Create an account below
  • Find the location in your neighborhood
  • Add what food you're planning to bring in your profile
  • Then come with your excess food, ready to swap for other peoples food.
